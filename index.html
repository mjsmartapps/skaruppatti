About Us

Welcome to S Karuppatti Store, your trusted source for authentic Palm Jaggery and traditional health foods. We are dedicated to reviving the traditional tastes of Tamil Nadu with a focus on purity, organic sourcing, and health benefits.

Founded in 2023, S Karuppatti Store began with a simple mission: to replace refined sugar with healthy, natural alternatives. We source our Karuppatti directly from farmers who use traditional extraction methods.
